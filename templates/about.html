Students Mental Health

-- Zach Richman --
As a student, mental health comes up a lot. But it is said to be said, stated to be stated, talked about just to be talked about. Nothing comes from it. A poster won't remove depression. A sign won't stop anxiety. What is done to talk about student mental health is non-existent. It exists, yes, but no one wants to change anything. Do anything about it. No one truly seems to care. 36.7% of children (Aged 12-17) have feelings of hopelessness. 8.9% attempt suicide. Is that not enough to warrant a bigger care about this? These things don't fix themselves. People need help. Lets stop making it so hard to get it. This site is meant to bring awareness to student mental health and that more needs to be done about it.
